About Me

David Azizi manages Hopkins Lab and contributes to an array of research projects. David specializes in computational and statistical approaches to social science researcg. David recieved a B.A. in Political Science from the University of Pennsylvania, where he minored in Survey Research and Data Analytics. While a student at Penn, he worked with the Program on Opinion Research and Election Studies, NBC Elections, and Prof. Marc Meredith on a project to update the process used to weight the National Election Poll exit poll.
